Frequently Asked Questions

Are our Breads really baked fresh each day?

Yes. Every morning we ensure that we bake a fresh set of our bread. We do not sell leftovers. After delivering to those who ordered, we keep the rest at the shop for sale. At the end of the day, the rest can either be given to the staff as take home or taken to orphanages to feed the children. We ensure to avoid waste as much as we can.

Do we Offer Door to Door Services?

Yes, we do. As at the start of the restaurant, we only sold at our retail shops as we were just starting out but as of now we have delivery agents. We try to have delivery agents in every town we have our retail shops so as to enable home or office deliveries based on our customers' preferences. All you need is contact the branch nearest to you.
